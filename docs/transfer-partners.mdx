# Airline transfer partners

These are the airline loyalty programmes that **American Express (Amex) UK Membership
Rewards (MR)** points can be moved into. Transfers are **one-way and irreversible**, so
confirm the ratio and that award space (a seat released for points) exists *before* you
transfer.

## How to read this table

- **Transfer ratio** — MR points *in* : airline miles *out*. `1 MR : 1 Avios` is
  one-to-one; `2 MR : 1 Skywards mile` and `3 MR : 2 KrisFlyer miles` are worse than
  one-to-one and need watching.
- **Estimated transfer time** — taken verbatim from Amex. "Instant" partners (British
  Airways, Virgin Atlantic) let you confirm award space first, *then* transfer. Slow
  partners (Singapore at up to 15 working days) mean you must speculatively transfer and
  hope the seat survives — much riskier.
- **Minimum transfer** — the smallest top-up Amex allows into that programme.

## The Avios cluster

**Three** of the Amex transfer partners above feed the **same** Avios currency at **1:1** —
British Airways Club, Iberia Club and Qatar Airways Privilege Club. Avios is *also* the
currency of Aer Lingus AerClub, but Aer Lingus is **not** a direct Amex transfer partner —
you reach it by moving Avios across, not by transferring MR points to it.

Because Avios can be moved *between* British Airways, Iberia, Qatar and Aer Lingus accounts,
transferring MR points into any one of the three Amex partners effectively gives you flexible
access to the whole Avios ecosystem. That breadth is why Avios is usually the default starting
point — see [Best-value airlines](/best-value-airlines).

## The catches

- **Sub-1:1 partners.** Emirates (2:1) and Singapore (3:2) halve / cut your points'
  headline count on transfer. They can still win if the redemption is rich enough — run
  the numbers in the [calculator](/expected-value).
- **Slow transfers = stranded points.** A 15-working-day transfer (Singapore) can miss
  the award seat entirely. Favour instant partners unless you have time to spare.
- **Minimums.** Most partners need 500–1,000 MR points minimum per transfer.

:::tip Default rule of thumb
If you don't yet know where you're flying, **don't transfer**. MR points are flexible;
airline miles are not. Transfer only once you've found bookable award space.
:::
