# Appendix — airlines beyond Amex UK

The search tools cover **more airlines than Amex UK transfers reach**. The programmes below are
tracked by seats.aero and SeatSpy but are **not** Amex UK Membership Rewards transfer partners —
so you can't get into them by transferring Amex points directly.

They're listed here because they're still useful if you hold **other** points currencies (for
example a US Amex, Chase, Capital One, or an airline co-brand card), or if you credit paid flights
to one of these programmes. For the Amex-UK-relevant view, see
[Finding award seats](/finding-flights).
