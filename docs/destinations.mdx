## Sweet-spot cheat sheet

| Trip | Programme | Why |
| --- | --- | --- |

| Transatlantic business | Virgin Atlantic (+ Delta) | Good value, instant transfer |

## By region

### Europe & short-haul
- **Avios (British Airways / Iberia)** — short-haul **Reward Flight Saver**: a fixed, small
  number of Avios plus a capped cash fee. The everyday workhorse for city breaks.
- **Flying Blue** — Air France/KLM hubs (Paris, Amsterdam) onward across Europe; watch
  **Promo Rewards** for discounted months.

### North America
- **Virgin Atlantic** — its own flights to New York, Boston, Miami, LA, plus **Delta One**
  (Delta's business class) partner space. Often the best business-class value transatlantic.
- **Avios via Qatar/Iberia** — lower-surcharge routing than British Airways on some US runs.

### Middle East & Asia
- **Qatar Airways (Avios) — Qsuite** business class via Doha to much of Asia, Africa and Australia.
- **Virgin Atlantic → ANA (All Nippon Airways)** — exceptional **Tokyo** first/business value
  (limited space, book the moment it opens).
- **Singapore KrisFlyer** — **Singapore Suites / business** to Singapore and onward Southeast Asia.
- **Cathay (Asia Miles)** — Hong Kong and onward Asia. *(Transfers paused 30 Jun 2026 4am–4pm BST
  / British Summer Time — see [partners](/transfer-partners).)*

### Australia, Africa & Latin America
- **Qantas Frequent Flyer** — the natural choice for Australia; strong **oneworld** partner space.
- **Flying Blue** / **Qatar (Avios)** — wide Africa coverage via Paris/Amsterdam and Doha.
- **Iberia (Avios)** — Madrid and onward Latin America.

:::tip Favour instant-transfer partners for peace of mind
Routes bookable through instant partners (Avios, Virgin) are far less stressful than ones needing
a slow speculative transfer. The whole point is quality of life.
:::
